## HYPERAKTIV

- **Description:** Open clinical CPT-II data used in the project’s initial exploratory analysis.
- **Credited source:** Hicks et al.; HYPERAKTIV dataset hosted through OSF.
- **Files used by the validator:** `patient_info.csv` and `CPT_II_ConnersContinuousPerformanceTest.csv`.
- **Source links used by the validator:**
  - <https://osf.io/download/hypj6/>
  - <https://osf.io/download/2gt3w/>
- **License and terms:** No repository-wide license grant for these data is asserted here. Verify the current OSF record, file notices, attribution requirements, and any applicable data-use or privacy terms before reuse or redistribution.
- **Important context:** The comparison group contains participants with other psychiatric diagnoses and is not a confirmed-healthy control group.

## BALLADEER

- **Description:** Behavioral and related measurements from diagnostically characterized participants performing a CPT/go-no-go-like attention task.
- **Citation:** Trujillo, Ferrer-Cascales, Teruel et al. (2026), *Scientific Data*.
- **Article DOI:** <https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-026-06758-7>
- **License and terms:** Verify the current dataset repository record and its attached terms before obtaining, reusing, or redistributing the data. This project does not grant rights in the BALLADEER source data.
- **Important context:** The completed v1.0.0 analysis is historical and methodologically qualified. The newer cluster-free candidate has not completed a real-data rerun.

## UCLA Consortium for Neuropsychiatric Phenomics / OpenNeuro `ds000030`

- **Description:** Phenotype metadata and Stop-Signal Task event data used for an adult cluster-free conceptual replication. MRI data were not required by this project.
- **Citation:** Bilder, Poldrack, Cannon, London, Freimer, Congdon, Karlsgodt, Sabb, and colleagues (2016), *Scientific Data*.
- **Article DOI:** <https://doi.org/10.1038/sdata.2016.110>
- **Dataset:** <https://openneuro.org/datasets/ds000030>
- **Dataset license:** Public Domain Dedication and License (PDDL), as identified by the OpenNeuro record at the time of analysis.
- **Reuse note:** Cite the dataset and its associated publication even where the applicable license does not require attribution. Check the current OpenNeuro record before reuse.

## ADHD Pupil Dataset / Figshare `7218725`

- **Description:** Pupil-size, eye-tracking, neuropsychological, reaction-time, accuracy, distractor-condition, and paired medication-session data from children with ADHD and controls.
- **Citation:** Rojas-Líbano, Wainstein, and Ossandón, “A pupil size, eye-tracking and neuropsychological dataset from ADHD children during a cognitive task,” *Scientific Data* (2019).
- **Article DOI:** <https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-019-0037-2>
- **Dataset DOI:** <https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.7218725>
- **Dataset license:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0), as identified by the Figshare record at the time of analysis.
- **Reuse note:** Follow the dataset’s attribution requirements and cite both the data descriptor and the Figshare record. Check the current Figshare record before reuse.
