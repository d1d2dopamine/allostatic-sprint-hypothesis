6. TEMPORAL SCOPE
  Four sequential blocks describe within-session changes over minutes only.
  They cannot test hypothesized phase transitions over weeks or months.

7. LIMITATIONS
  - Stop-Signal is not the same task as BALLADEER or CPT-II; this is a conceptual replication.
  - Adaptive stop-signal procedures target ~50%% stopping success; stop-success rate is not a primary outcome.
  - SSRT is reported only when trial-level SSD and sufficient data permit integration-method estimation.
  - GMM clusters are exploratory unless independently preregistered and replicated.
  - Behavioral patterns do not validate dopamine, ATP, receptor, metabolic, or neural mechanisms.
  - Cross-sectional data cannot establish cyclical within-person states.
